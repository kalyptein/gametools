tags
* reach: can attack from the 2nd rank, -2 attack if ally in front is equal size or larger, or using a 2h weapon, no penalty vs mounted or elevated enemies
* brace: a hit against a charging target deals 2x damage
* armor piercing: +2 attack vs non-magic metal armor or with hard shells/carapaces
* loading: requires a full turn (action, no movement) to load
* charge: charge attacks deal 2x damage
* missile: provokes when used, can't be fired at targets within 5' (+1 / +0 / -2 attack at range bands)
	* use WIS for attack
* thrown: doesn't provoke, can be used from offhand
	* use WIS or DEX for attack
	* medium or large can add +STR damage
* 2h: can be carried in one hand, but requires two hands to use in combat
* sweep: can be used to "dual wield" without a second weapon?  grants Cleave?
* splash (near miss = half damage splash, hit = full damage for 2 rounds)

* small melee
	* can use +STR or +DEX on attack; no +STR damage bonus
* finesse melee
	* can use +STR or +DEX on attack; can only use +STR for damage

* carry 100 coins for free, then 1 slot per 100 coins

load
* max 10 equipped slots
	* quick access, in / ready for use
* max 16 stowed slots
	* 10 slots per large container (backpack, sack)
		* 1 backpack, 2 sacks (1 per hand)
		* containers are 0 slots when deployed, 1 slot when stowed
	* retrieving a stowed item: WIS check per round
* load
	* use the heaviest load between ready and stowed slots
	* modify the load stowed limits by CON
	* none (0-3 ready, 0-8 stowed): 40' move
	* light (4-5 ready, 9-12 stowed): 30' move
	* medium (6-7 ready, 13-14 stowed): 20' move
	* heavy (8-10 ready, 15-16 stowed): 10' move

penalty to climb, swim, sneak, jump
* worse of armor weight penalty or load (read load weight as armor weight)
* sneaking: -1 for medium, -2 for heavy
* CON checks for fatigue: -1 for medium, -2 for heavy (forced march, sleep rough, etc)

mounts and vehicles
