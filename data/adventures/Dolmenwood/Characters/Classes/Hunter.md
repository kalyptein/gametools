* HD: d8 (+2 per paragon)
* Light and medium armor
* Simple melee weapons, all ranged weapons

Marksman
* Gain (LVL+1) to attack and multiple attacks when fighting with thrown and missile attacks.
	* Attacks: 1.5 (3rd), 2 (5th), 2.5 (7th), 3 (9th)
	* A "half attack" is rolled normally but deals half damage, rounded up.
* If your shot strikes a target that was unaware of danger, +1d damage.

Apothecary
* You know the salve, restorative, curative, stimulant, and foebane recipes.

Wildcraft
* You are trained in:
	* Navigation, pathfinding, avoiding natural hazards and obstacles, mapping
	* Stealth, spotting the hidden, sensing danger
	* Tracking, counter-tracking
	* Bushcraft (shelter-building, finding water, traps and snares, etc)
	* Plant and animal lore, foraging, hunting, fishing, animal handing and training

Trophies
* When you take a trophy from a new kind of creature, or a noteworthy exemplar, gain Luck.
* When you have taken a trophy from from a dangerous beast or monster (mundane or magical) you slew, once per Long Rest, when dealing with that kind of creature, choose 1:
	* Gain Inspiration to use against it (fighting, tracking, etc).
	* Gain Resistance (5) to its attacks and abilities (Reaction, WIS DC 12 to Sustain each round).
* Trophies must either be carried (1 slot) or displayed in your home (house, hut, wagon, tent, etc).

Nocturnal
* Your keen senses allow you to operate in darkness as if you could perceive Nearby.
	* You can't read, see, detail or anything else that would require light, but can detect unhidden creatures, navigate without tripping or bumping into things, etc.
	* You can target Nearby creatures with ranged attacks if there is at least a trace of light.


Means of Introduction
* One day you saw a trophy of a rare and exotic monster from Dolmenwood and felt the call of adventure.


Talents
* Mixology (choose 1)
	* Brew Potions like a Magician.
	* Gain Apothecary and learn 2 recipes
	* Training in poisons (with Apothecary, also learn 2 poison recipes).
	* (with Apothecary) +A when making 2 recipes.
* Heroic Capability (choose 1)
	* Gain the fighter's Swift, Cunning, or Implacable.
* Animal Companion
	* As a downtime action, forge a connection with an animal up to about the size of a deer or hound.  It is loyal, clever, and fearless in aiding you.  It gains +LVL to its own levels (max of your level).
* Pack-Rat
	* When you expend a use of a consumable item carried since your last Long Rest (arrows, oil, rations), roll d6.  On a 5+, regain that use.
	* You have a 4th Small free slot.
* Merry Man
	* Gain 2 sub-disciplines from Skullduggery.
* Fey Trophy
	* You can gain crests for a single Faerie Rune.  Start with 1 Knave's Crest that you crafted from part of a faerie beast.  You are Bound By Faerie Law.
		* Choose from the following Runes: Horned, Bleak, Gossamer, Blossoming, Unkind, Unyielding, Whiskered, Jaundiced, or Greening.
* Many Skins
	* Once per Long Rest, cast a Trophy into the fire and take the form of the creature (up to your Level), until you change back or sleep.
* Slayer's Oath
	* If you swear aloud to kill a specific being, roll damage against it with advantage, and treat yourself as if you had a Trophy of it.
	* If you abandon your oath, you can't use Luck or Inspiration, or take a new oath for one month, and you can never swear against that specific target ever again.
* Headhunter
	* You can use each Trophy for Inspiration twice per Long Rest.
* Gwyrigon's Gift
	* You are follower of Gwyrae.  Form a pact with a Gwyrigon and gain access to one arcane spell (up to Tier 3) fitting its aspect.  Cast with INT, WIS, CON, or CHA.

Bonus
* Add 2 among Dexterity, Wisdom and Constitution.
* +1 to ranged attacks or +1 to weapon damage
* +1 to melee attacks or +1 to weapon damage
* Gain a Racial Bonus
