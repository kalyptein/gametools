* HD: d6 (+1 per paragon)
* Light and medium armor, shields
* Light and medium Weapons

Bardic Arts
* Training in oration, music, acting, deception, rumor-mongering, diplomacy, and charm.
* +1 to Carousing rolls (for everyone in your group)

Lore
* Training in recalling lore and deciphering documents.
* Start with 2 extra languages.
* On first hearing of a subject with some claim to fame, roll INT DC 15 (training applies, DC can vary with fame/obscurity) to know some snippet of lore about it (perhaps partial, slanted, or cryptic, but not outright false).
	* e.g. a person, group, place, event, monster, deity, magic item, etc

Inspire
* With inspiring words, music, or deeds, you can bestow Inspiration to allies (CHA) times per Long Rest.

Aegis of Song
* While you play music, sing or recite poetry, those Nearby are immune to magic based on music or language and you hedge out magical silence.
	* e.g. charm, command, hypnotic music, but not violent sound, like thunder

Fascinate (CHA, Tier 2, Safe, Heavy Focus (10 minutes, or round if begun in combat), Nearby listeners)
* You weave hypnotic music, song, oration, or poetry, enthralling listeners.
	* P [1 + CL/2] vs R [(TL-1) + (CHA | WIS)]
* Enthralled
	* You can walk, and the enthralled will follow you.
	* The trance is immediately broken by harm, obvious danger, loud noises, etc.
	* At performance's end, individuals roll (CHA | WIS) DC 15 each round to break the trance.
	* Non-hostile subjects enthralled for at least 30 minutes are charmed (regarding you as a good friend for the next hour) or lulled to sleep, depending on your choice of music.


Talents
* Virtuoso: gain +A to Fascinate.
* Stray Talent: learn one arcane spell (not Maleficare) from a scroll you've successfully used.
* Faerie Friend: you can gain crests for a single Faerie Rune.  Start with 1 crest, either Knave's, Knight's, or King's.  You are Bound By Faerie Law.
	* If King's crest, then some lord of the realm has taken a particular interest in you.
* Life on the Road: become trained in two sub-disciplines from Thievery and/or Wildcraft.
* Gain Apothecary and learn 2 recipes 
	* Training in poisons.
* Of the People: +A to downtime checks involving people, society, or art (excluding carousing), improve to +2 carousing
* Warrior-Poet: when you or a Nearby ally fells a foe, unleash a verse healing them 1d4 Guard.
* Music's Aegis: while singing your Countersong, you personally have +A to resist all magic.
* Sense Magic like a Magician.
* Brew Potions like a Magician.

Bonuses
* Add 2 stat points to same or different stats.
* +1 to melee and ranged attacks
* +1 to cast Fascinate and +1 Potency
