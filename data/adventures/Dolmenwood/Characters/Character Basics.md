## Level

* 0th - 10th
	* Above 10th, gain paragon ranks, with different benefits.
* Level MOD: LVL = level / 2, rounding down (+0 to +5)

## Stats

* For adventurers, stats range from 3 to 18 (may go higher for monsters).
* Stat MOD = (stat value - 10) / 2, rounding down (-4 to +4)
	* STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA
* Stats can take damage from various effects (poison, magic, wounds, etc).
	* This can cause a stat's MOD to change, as well as other things calculated from it.

Stats
* Strength
	* Feats of strength, athletics, rough climbing, swimming
	* Wield forceful weapons, grapple.
	* Stand fast against pushes, throws, etc, pull free from grapples and entanglement
	* Carry gear

	* Stealth, sleigh of hand, tinkering, sheer climbing, acrobatics, contortionism
	* Wield finesse weapons.
	* Improve your defense when not in Heavy armor.
	* Dive for cover, keep your footing and balance, react quickly to danger, evade beams and blasts, slip out of bonds and grapples
* Constitution
	* Endure poison, disease, bodily curses, privation, and fatigue
	* Take hits and recover from harm
	* Carry gear
* Intelligence
	* Recall and apply lore and the cunning arts, feats of memory and cogitation, decipher clues and mysteries, study and learn
	* Counter malign spells and magical effects when you have a protective talisman or implement at the ready

	* Inspire, intimidate, command, forge rapport, charm, negotiate, carouse, exert willpower, show discipline
	* Resist fear, resist attempts to overwhelm or afflict your will and spirit, brush off social attacks and embarrassments

## Combat

### Attack Bonus

* Stat bonus portion of attack bonus max +4; class bonus is added after.
	* Forceful (including forceful thrown): STR + 1 if DEX >= +1
	* Finesse (including finesse thrown): DEX + 1 if STR >= +1
	* Missile (including finesse thrown): WIS + 1 if DEX >= +1

### Defense Bonus

* Unarmored: DEX-1
* Light armor: DEX
* Medium armor: DEX+2
* Heavy armor: +4
* Shield: +2

### Hit Points

* Total HP= (max of 1dHD) + CON + (level-1)dHD
	* Reroll HP at each level, taking the new value if it's higher than current.  Otherwise take current HP + 1 (unless that would be above the max possible).
* Split HP into two equal pools, Guard and Grit, with any odd point going to Guard.

## Magic

Casting Bonus
* Stat bonus portion of casting bonus max +4; class bonus is added after.
	* Arcane: INT + 1 if WIS | CHA >= +1
	* Holy: CHA + 1 if WIS >= +1
	* Faerie: CHA + 1 if INT | WIS | CON >= +1

### Encumbrance

* Readied and Stowed
	* Readied
		* Items in Free slots (unless noted) are Readied, and up to 4 other slots-worth of items can be designated Readied, accessible a a moment's notice.
		* This should include weapons, shield, ammo, potions, scrolls, wands, etc.
	* Stowed
		* Non-Readied items are Stowed and require action(s) to unearth in a hurry.
		* If you have a Heavy Load, Stowed items are so tightly packed and bound up that they take a minute to access.
* Load
	* Free Slots
		* Clothing and personal effects.
		* Worn items (crown, helm, belt, boots, gloves/gauntlets, cloak, amulets, etc).
		* A worn backpack (items in a pack are Stowed).
		* 3 Small items in belt or bandolier.
		* Up to 100 coins in a pouch.
		* Any reasonable number of Petty items (Stowed).
	* Light Load
		* Up to 6 slots.
		* +A to outrun / out-maneuver those w/ heavier loads or of Large or larger Size.
	* Medium Load
		* Up 16 + (STR | CON) slots.
		* +D to swim and sheer climb.
	* Heavy Load
		* Up to 22+(2 x STR | CON) slots.
		* Can't swim or sheer climb, +D to stat checks, attacks, resists, and casting, but not defense.
	* Overburdened
		* Greater than a Heavy Load.

### Luck & Inspiration

* Luck
	* During an action, you can spend a Luck to reroll a die (d20 or damage), keeping whichever result you prefer.
	* Luck can be spent on behalf of allies.
* Gaining Luck
	* The GM can award Luck for various achievements: heroism, self-sacrifice, brilliant plans, moments of awesome, etc.
	* When you roll a natural 1 and suffer a critical failure, gain a Luck.
	* After a Long Rest, if you have >1 Luck, reduce to 1.
	* When you finish a Respite, if you have 0 Luck, gain 1.
* Inspiration
	* Inspiration is granted by spells, bardic inspiration, etc, and is spent like Luck.
	* You can only have 1 Inspiration at a time, and it is lost after a Long Rest.
