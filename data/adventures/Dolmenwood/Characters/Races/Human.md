Ambitious
* Gain an extra talent at 1st level.
	* This can be a glamour, indicating a trace of faerie blood.  Later talents can be glamours as well, but only if you took a glamour at 1st level.

Choose 1:
* Red-handed: +1 to attack with melee and ranged weapons.
* Seeker of Power: +1 to spellcasting checks.

Earthborn
* Heed your instincts to briefly sense Nearby supernatural / otherworldly presences or forces (hauntings, holiness, corruption), gaining a vibe of what and a rough impression of where.  Roll WIS DC 11; on failure, your inner eye is dimmed until after a Long Rest.



Talents
* Gain 2 Glamours (if able to use glamours).
* Black Powder Herald
	* You've stormed the borders of Faerie with flame and iron.  You can use firearms and explosives, and are trained in gunsmithing.
* Namer of Beasts
	* You are trained in handling and training animals.
	* Animal Companion
		* As a downtime action, forge a connection with an animal up to about the size of a deer or hound.  It is loyal, clever, and fearless in aiding you.  It gains +LVL to its own levels (max of your level).





Means of Introduction
* You just went to Dolmenwood for reasons.
* A trace of fey or Lyonessan blood drew you to Dolmenwood.
* Chasing after tales of wonder and adventure brought you here.
* You were hired as an agent of an external occult order (perhaps not openly) to seek Dolmenwood and procure occult lore, materials, and objects.
* You found a pilgrim's token from an ancestor's visit to the Cathedral of Saint Clewyd and decide to follow in their footsteps.
* You found a treasure map to some place called Dolmenwood.
