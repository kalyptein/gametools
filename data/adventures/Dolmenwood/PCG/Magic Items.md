
Origin
* 1-2) Holy
	* Blessed Consumable
	* Saint's Relic
* 3-4) Faerie
	* Specific creator (a lord or lady or other famous fey)
	* Fey race (goblins, elves, grimalkin, etc, but not unique to a specific individual)
	* Realm (one of the faerie realms, or just faerie in general, but not directly due to its lord or major inhabitants)
* 5-6) Arcane
	* 1-2) Drune
	* 3-4) Witch / Wood God
	* 5-6) Unaffiliated
		* Could be made from meteoric iron, some part of a magical beast, was involved in a powerful event that imbued it, made by an unaffiliated mage or arcane craftsman, etc.

Relics
* Types
	* Bodily part of saint
	* Contains bodily part of saint or token central to their miracle or deeds
		* Contains a saint's tooth, has a nail from the crucifixion forged into it
	* Item central to their miracle or deeds
* All Relics
	* If it's a relic of one of the major saints, holy casters can use their miracle while holding it.
* Minor Relics
	* Just counts as a relic.
* Major Relics
	* Full-fledged magic items, as well as relics.
	* May allow a holy caster an extra daily use of the miracle or an upcast casting of the miracle using their own slot.
		* May allow non-caster faithful limited access to the miracle (once per interval, with some cost, only in times of great need, etc)
