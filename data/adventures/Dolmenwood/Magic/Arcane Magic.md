Reasearch Points and Insight
* You gain arcane research points (research) by studying magical phenomena.
* You  may also gain arcane insight (insight) as well, which are aspected in various ways.
	* Power: a special kind of insight needed to invent spells of greater magnitude.
		* Impressive feats of magic (breaking a powerful curse, a super-effective fireball usage, etc) might award a power insight all by itself, without research.
	* Fire, Water, Protection, Illusion, War, etc.

Inventing Spells
* Cost
	* Lesser (2 research, 3 days)
	* Greater (4? research, 1 power insight, 1 week)
	* Mighty (6? research, 2 power insight, 2 weeks)
* Once the time is spent, you spend the cost and roll the random spell which you invented.
	* Time spent should be in town or similar sanctuary, but it doesn't require single-minded focus.
	* You can gain a free insight (aspected or power) based on special conditions of the area you do your research in (ley lines, dolmens, faerie lands, magical phenomena, lunar phase, season, weather, etc).  If not used in this work, it is lost.
	* You don't reinvent spells you already know; reroll until you have a new one.
	* Invention roll event:
		* Normal invention.
		* You invent a spell of lower magnitude.  Only spend the cost for that.
		* Refund 1 insight or power key.
		* There's a mishap or discovery as part of the process.
		* You generate a key with an aspect of your choice to be used immediately with this round of invention (can't be saved).
		* Roll 1-2 extra spells to pick from.
	* DM rolls 3 candidate spells.  You will get the first one normally.  If you spend an aspected insight appropriate to one of the other spells, you can choose it instead.
		* You may also spend an aspected insight to "reserve" a spell you rolled but didn't select.  It will appear as one of the three rolls in your next invention.  If it's the first, you can pick it for free; if it's the second or third, you would need to spend another insight to pick it.

Finding Manifestations to Commune With
* using Detect Magic while consuming the fresh, wild products of a region (wild plants/fungi, fresh game, water, sometimes even breathing the air) lets you detect the taste of a wood god when you're in their general territory; stronger as you get closer.  the taste will be strongest / clearest in the product most aligned with their nature (plants for a god of the forest, animal flesh for a god of beasts, water for a water god, etc)
