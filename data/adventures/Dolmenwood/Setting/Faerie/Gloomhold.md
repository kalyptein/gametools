	* The Blind King
		* A terrible iron-crowned giant with grey, sagging skin, hair stiff with frost, and empty eye sockets. Though sightless, the Blind King senses the echoes of all that comes to pass within his drear realm, Gloomhold. All of the King’s servants are blind, for his countenance brings eternal death.  The Blind King has no contact whatsoever with Dolmenwood and mercilessly imprisons any mortals who trespass in his realm.
