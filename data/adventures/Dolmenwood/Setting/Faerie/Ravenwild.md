## Inhabitants

### Prince Mallowheart

* Ruler of Ravenwild, also known as Prince Seven-Past-Noon. A decadent, cruel, and capricious frost elf who regards mortals as mere playthings.
* His sallow lips and skin are blackened by frostbite. He dresses in robes of rich fur and wears a crown of iron thorns and fused diamonds.
* Mallowheart is the estranged half-brother of the Cold Prince (p30) and foster father of the magician Ygraine Mordlin (p84).

* Prince Mallowheart escaped Frigia and his brother the Cold Prince during their estrangement, when he was cast into prison.  By attuning to this rune and becoming master of the formerly empty Ravenwild, he escaped and now has the power to come and go in Dolmenwood, where his brother does not.

	* The Drune may fear his meddling, recognizing his cunning.
	* His fae lore is what allowed him to bind to Ravenwild, a feat inaccessible to so many other would-be lords.
