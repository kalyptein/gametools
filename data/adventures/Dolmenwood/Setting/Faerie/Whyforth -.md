## Inhabitants

### The Earl of Yellow

* A mirthful, ochre-skinned satyr with horns of shimmering ivory and a mane of radiant gold.
* The Earl rules the realm of Whyforth and a portion of central Dolmenwood.
* He seldom visits the mortal world himself, but takes an active interest in the doings of mortal folk in his dominion.
* He rules from Spoondrift Castle, a towering fortification of spiralled, yellow sandstone—seemingly carved from a single piece, by the whimsy of the wind.

Mortal Dominion—The Yellow Wood & Prigwort
* The Earl’s dominion also encompasses part of central Dolmenwood (hexes 1106, 1205, 1306, 1406), known as the Yellow Wood.
* The Earl’s emissaries—enigmatic elf knights clad entirely in yellow and riding atop great golden wolves—patrol this area of Dolmenwood and visit Prigwort in secret.
* The town of Prigwort (hex 1106) is located within the Yellow Wood and its folk secretly pay fealty to the Earl of Yellow. If the town were ever seriously threatened, the Earl would certainly lend his aid.
	* In particular, the Elevated Council of Brewmasters secretly pay him homage and can call on him.  They pay tribute in a portion of Prigwort's finest spirits and brews.
		* The Earl protects the town and grants the brewmasters fabulous ingredients from Faerie to include in their beverages.
	* Common folk often speak of the Earl, tell stories of him, etc, but don't realize they are considered his vassals.

* There is a folklore figure "Lord Bag-in-Hand" who wanted to establish a manor outside Prigwort.  He build a strange wall with inset mirrors (The Bagwall), then vanished.  Some claim he was the Earl of Yellow in disguise, playing a prank of granting some mysterious boon to Prigwort.
