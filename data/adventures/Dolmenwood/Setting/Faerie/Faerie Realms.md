	* Ravenwild

* 6) The White Way
	* Lampwrack, Tallowspire, Gloomhold
		* Adding Gloomhold in location 6 (utter darkness; the gate lies hidden within)

* Frigia
	* Accessed from the sealed paths within the Ring of Chell and at each Summerstone (sealed, unless disturbed)
* Hypnagogia
	* Reached by falling asleep on any Faerie Road, or by sleeping under the influence of certain fey-touched drugs or magics, or in certain places, in the mortal world.
* The Baths of Astralon
	* The Baths move.  They can appear as a location along any Faerie Road, doors can open in Dolmenwood (or other lands/realms), and it can be summoned with the correct rites.
