
* aka the Table of Chell
	* the leylines are named in the old language of the Drune, including Chell
* The glade exists within a pocket realm created by the power of the Summerstones, stamped with he images of their beginning.

* A pleasant glade, springy soft grass, always summery
* A standing stone rises in the center (a manifestion of Drodh, the central Summerstone)
	* At its base, an ancient slab of stone with cryptic geometric lines and dots carved on it, neolithic, sits upon a trio of stone "legs" at belly-height.
* Misty forest beyond.  Above the tree rise 6, mist-shrouded standing stones
	* Each is one of the Summerstones; each is solitary, except one that is three (the 3 sisters of the Pelloryons)

* Statues ring the glade, facing the table.  Symbolic images of the folk who joined to oppose the Cold Prince and liberate Dolmenwood, swearing to their purpose here on Summer's Table.
	* A king and his knights (arthur)
	* A bishop (the church) and disciples, monks, nuns, and other clergy
	* Robed occult figures (Drune), with Merlin / Myrddin beside
	* Occult women (witches), with Nimue beside
		* Merlin and Nimue stand beside each other, with their groups spreading out from their flank
	* Breggle lords and knights
	* Various eclectic fey; free folk who opposed the Cold Prince

* A path leads away, back to the world.  Once followed, it can't be found again to return.
