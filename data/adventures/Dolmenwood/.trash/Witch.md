	- interact with its presence on the map, deal with a relic, mix up a special brew from rare/special stuff and vision-quest, (Lesser only) tap into a ley line through a nodal stone (gives Lesser bond with a patron thematically aligned with the ley line's flavor)

	- each god has a set of spells in the Lesser, Greater, and/or Mighty tiers.
	- you have a "bond level" with the god (Lesser, Greater, Mighty) that grants access to that tier and below.  So you can do minor stuff and get limited access.

* a witch could commune with other arcane beings besides the wood gods, such as the Nag-Lord, the Flayed Queen, eldritch constellations, etc (but not holy beings or faerie lords).  They would get appropriate spells.  This likely brings corruptive side-effects.
* a witch can swear fealty to an arcane being, gaining more spells and powers, but eschewing other spells and powers.  PC witches are assumed not to do this as their life would be consumed with service to the being.


Communing
* Ritual: every wood god has a ritual that grants Lesser Communion.
	* Can be devised by studying the god, local witches will likely know it.
	* Requires calling on the god by name
	* usually involves a psychedelic brew made from herbs / fungi / animal or monsters parts from the area of its domain, or
	* might require a certain phase of the moon, time of year, location (beneath an oak tree, etc) or other ritual actions
* Map Features
* Direct Interaction


* Rite of Dreaming
	* A nocturnal, psychedelic ritual under auspicious conditions that allows the witch to touch a Gwyrigon and form a bond with it.
		* Always requires a psychedelic as a base element.
	* Requires at least 3 points of sacraments of a given tier (Lesser, Greater, Might) to form a bond of that tier.
		* General Sacraments
			* Magic Items
				* consumables are used up and 1 charge must be spent from a charged item
				* minor items / most consumables: Lesser
				* major items: Greater
				* artifacts: Mighty
			* Heart of an Aspected Monster
				* or brain, core, etc
				* Level 5- (Lesser), Level 6+ (Greater), Avatar (Mighty)
			* Touch of the Gwyrigon (once gained, must complete the ritual within a moon)

				* intimate, non-violent physical contact
				* a piece of the Gwyrigon's body, consumed (blood, flesh, etc) or worked into the creation of the ritual brew (stirred with a claw, etc)


Finding Manifestations to Commune With
* using Detect Magic while consuming the fresh, wild products of a region (wild plants/fungi, fresh game, water, sometimes even breathing the air) lets you detect the taste of a wood god when you're in their general territory; stronger as you get closer.  the taste will be strongest / clearest in the product most aligned with their nature (plants for a god of the forest, animal flesh for a god of beasts, water for a water god, etc)
* in cases where local folk maintain customs that tap into the god, the perception is quite noticeable (smell in the smoke of ritual bonfires, in specially brewed ale, etc), whereas normally civilization erases the taste (fields tilled with iron, the ring of church bells, water stirred by boat hulls, etc)
* the taste of herbs and fungi (and animal / monster flesh) that could be part of a communion brew is evident.
