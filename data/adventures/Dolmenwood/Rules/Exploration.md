
## Recovery

Regaining HP
* spend reserve to restore Guard and Grit after a short rest
* spend reserve to restore Guard and Grit after a long rest, getting 1dHD+CON for free

Regaining Stats
* regain 1 stat point (per stat?  across all stats?)

Regaining Reserve
* 1 per long rest, 2 for a respite

Regaining Spells
* ginder mode: only regain 1d4 spells after a long rest
	* maybe allow regaining 1 spell after short, but only once per long rest

* Guard, Grit 
	* refresh after a short rest, 1 reserve per d6, can't restore grit if not Fresh
	* refresh after a long rest, 1 reserve per d6
	* if the HD rolls more HP that you need, doesn't cost stamina
* Reserve
	* Reserve = 2 x Constitution
	* regain 1 stamina per long rest, 2/long rest of respite
	* 
	* most magic healing spells heals in d6s and costs 1 stamina for each (unless if overflows HP)
		* magic healing helps in combat/time pressure, and gets fallen people back up, but doesn't extend HP
	* wounds require medical skill and time as well (healing magic can help replace need for skill and tools, and compensate for poor conditions, but doesn't speed things up further)




reactions
* 2d6+CHA
* 0-6 = hostile
* 7-8 = suspicious
* 9 = neutral
* 10-11 = curious
* 12+ = friendly


Traps
* given time, training, and tools, auto-success on bypassing/disabling
