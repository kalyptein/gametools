## Survival

* Choose whether to fish, forage, or hunt for the day, alongside normal travel.
	* If spending the whole day looking for food (not traveling), take +A.
* A single character rolls WIS (Wildcraft) DC 18.
	* If the party splits up, each group can roll, but also checks for getting lost and encounters.
	* Modifiers can apply for season, location, etc.

Fishing
* Requires Fishing Gear (1 slot).
* Can be done in any hex with a lake or river.
* Success: 2d6 rations.  Roll for type of catch.

Foraging
* Success: 1d6 rations (1d4 in winter, 1d8 in autumn).  Roll for type of find (even odds of finding plants or fungi, roll type).
	* In hexes with special herbs or fungi, they may also be found.

Hunting
* Success: you have found wild game.  Roll for type and number of quarry.
	* Roll DEX (Stealth) DC (11+animal's WIS) to sneak up Nearby.  On failure, you are Far.
	* You must kill the animal, and have surprise on your attack.  Ordinary beasts are likely to flee when able.
	* Small animals yield 1 ration per HP, Medium animals yield 2 rations per HP, and Large animals yield 4 rations per HP.

Journeying

* weather
	* weather type can cause one or more effects:
		* Impeded: -2 progress for the day when traveling
		* Obscuring: -1 to rolls to avoid becoming lost, half normal starting distance for encounters, light obscurement is omnipresent
		* Wet: +D to to firebuilding
		* Cold: of varying levels, may impose poor conditions on rest or even cause harm to the underprotected.
* getting lost
	* while following a road or river, with a reliable guide, or toward an obvious landmark, there is no chance of getting lost
	* each day, roll 1d6 against the chance of getting lost based on terrain.
		* light terrain (1-in-6) or along a (sub-road) trail in heavier terrain
		* medium terrain (2-in-6)
		* heavy terrain (3-in-6)
	* if the party becomes lost, the character in charge of navigation can roll WIS (Wildcraft) DC 18 to notice and correct course.  If they beat DC 15, they have a suspicion they're off course, but don't know how to correct this.
		* The party remains lost until able to locate themselves via a road, river, landmark, etc., but can try to head in specific a direction to make this happen (east, north, etc; with a chance of getting lost again and failing to do this).
