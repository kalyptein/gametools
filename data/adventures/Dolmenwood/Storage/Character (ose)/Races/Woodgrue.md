
* demi-fey: they can take glamours as feats


Bat Ears
* +1 to Listen checks.

Small
* can't wield large weapons
* armor must be tailored to your stature
* +2 defense against Large or larger creatures, while able to move freely.

Moon Sight
* As the glamour.

Vulnerable to Cold Iron
* applies to iron/steel; specially crafted "cold iron" is above and beyond this
- +1 damage per die from iron
- can handle iron (it's unpleasant), but can't wear iron/steel armor (too itchy)
	- can use bronze, etc armor

Musical Weaponry
* can use music instruments as d4 weapons without damaging them
* small instruments do d4, medium ones do d6; you actually hit the target, so can be wielded 1h
* can also deal ranged damage with their music, single target, 30' range, same as melee
	* wind deals piercing, stringed deals slashing, percussion deals bludgeoning
	* for ranged, you have to play, so 2h

Secular
* Can't be Clerics or Friars, but don't suffer the full extent of the faerie's Outside the Bounds of Heaven.

Compulsive Jubilation
* On witnessing a feast, party, celebration, or festival, you feel compelled to partake.  Save vs spells to resist, if you must, but you feel dejected and lethargic until a Long Rest (-2 to checks).
	* if you do it with a sufficiently grand spread, you earn revelry tokens, spend for a reroll?  max 2?
* You can consume unlimited amounts of intoxicants.  You become extremely drunk/high, but never actually collapse, blackout, etc.

Mad Revelry
* 1/day, play an enchanted tune on a woodwind while whooping and dancing manically.
	* Affects all within 30', who must save vs spells.  Lasts as long as you play.  You cannot take other actions, and can only move at quarter speed.
* Choose a melody:
	* Confide: drunkenly slur out a deeply held emotion or reveal a secret.
	* Dance: dance a profane jig, gaining +1 defense but unable to move from their location.
	* Imbibe: ravenously consume and liquids (even if not potable), herbs, mushrooms, and similar without reach and act incredibly drunk (-2 attack).
	* Jape: mock the immediately preceding words, act, or event.
	* Jubilate: burst into uncontrollable laughter, unable to speak (1-in-6 per round of falling down).
	* Mount: try to mount a nearby creature and ride it piggyback (save vs hold to resist being mounted).  If mounted, go along with it if under the spell, otherwise save vs hold to buck off an undesired rider.
	* Revel: cannot speak, only bark, whoop, howl, etc only with the melody.  Slowed unless moving toward the woodgrue.
