
Estray Form
* Child-sized humanoid cat.
* Can talk, has hands, use magic, etc.

Keen Ears
* +1 to Hearing checks

Small
* can't wield large weapons
* armor must be tailored to your stature
* +2 defense against Large or larger creatures, while able to move freely.

Devour Rodents
* Can magically consume rodents, giant and normal, in endless quantities.
* Apart from providing sustenance, each fresh giant rat or rough-dozen of normal rats heals 1 hp.

Shapechanging
* Change shape as an action (completed at end of round) to Chester (unlimited) or Wilder (1/day, when at half hp or less and presently in danger) forms.
	* You can't transform while shackled or caged in iron.
	* Chester: a large cat.
		* Small, defense 12, speed 30 (40?), claw/claw/bite multiattack (1 damage each)
		* Can squeeze through small gaps, etc like a cat.  +1 to climbing.
		* No falling damage, as long as you're free to land on your feet.
		* Have the Moon Sight glamour.
		* Can't speak (just yowl/meow/hiss), can't use magic (including most glamours), no hands.
		* Inventory merges into body and is unavailable.  Items beyond carrying capacity are dropped.
		* Turn back as an action, but only when unobserved.
	* Wilder: half-seen, feral 3' high cheshire cat with fur standing up as if electrified.
		* On transforming, heal 2d6 hp.
		* Half-seen: mostly just visible as a pair of deranged cat-eyes and fangs.  +2 defense
		* Small, defense 13, speed 30 (40?), +2 attack, claw/claw/bite multiattack (1d4 damage each)
		* Fight or Flight: you begin in Fight mode, and can change to Flight at will, but can't switch back.
			* Fight: indiscriminate blender that can't tell friend from foe.
			* Flight: immediately seeks to flee for maximum person safety without any regard for friends, goals, dignity, etc.
		* Lasts 2d4 rounds, after which you return instantly to Estray form.

Classes
* Common: Bard, Enchanter, Hunter, Thief
* Rare: Knight (plenty of fey knights, few sworn to mortal lords as this class implies)
* Forbidden: Cleric, Friar
