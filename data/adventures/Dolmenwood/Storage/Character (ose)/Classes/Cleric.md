Languages

Holy Orders
* St Faxis
	* Arcane Antipathy
		* +2 saves vs arcane magic and powers of arcane beings (demons, elementals, etc)
		* arcane casters and being (demons, elementals) have -2 save vs your spells
	* can use Detect Magic to sense arcane magic of all types as well
* St Sedge
	* Lay on Hands
		* 1/day heal (Level) hp or end a temporary status (like Lesser Healing) with a touch.  Can't be interrupted like a spell, can move and use same turn.
	* Aura of Zeal
		* ??? courage aura
* St Signis
	* Undead Slayer
		* +1 attack and damage vs undead
		* your attacks harm undead as if using a magic or silver weapon
		* +2 saves vs necromancy and the powers of the dead
	* can use Detect Magic to sense necromantic power, the presence of undead (their passage, victims slain by undead, necromantic spells, etc)
	* Lay to Rest
		* dead you personally lay to rest via proper burial and prayer are subject to a Turn by you if they would rise.  If turned, they don't rise.  Doesn't affect the already undead
			* this protection automatically applies to you, even if not properly buried
