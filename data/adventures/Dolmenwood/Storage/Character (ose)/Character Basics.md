

bucket stat rolling (net +3 MOD)
- all stats start at -2 MOD
- roll to assign 1x +3, 3x +2 MOD and 3x +1 MOD
	- if a stat exceeds +3 MOD, roll to assign the excess
- choose: assign +2 MOD and +1 MOD to separate stats OR roll to assign +2 and +1 and then choose 2 stats to swap
- determine actual stat from MOD
	-2 MOD = 5
	-1 MOD = 5 + 1d3
	+0 MOD = 8 + 1d4
	+1 MOD = 12 + 1d3
	+2 MOD = 15 + 1d2
	+3 MOD = 18


Saves
* Types
	* Doom: effects that kill or inflict physical malignites; poison, death magic, disease.
		* Also used to check for misfortune and bad luck on some skill checks, and for carried item saves against damage or destruction.
	* Ray: directional effects that can be dodged, such as lightning bolts or magical rays.
	* Hold: effects that hinder or prevent movement; paralysis, entanglement, pinning, petrification
	* Blast: effects that fill an area; dragon breath, explosions
	* Spell: magical effects that act directly upon you, rather than through some intermediary; curses, ensorcellment, glamours, etc
* Rolls
	* Natural 1 always fails; natural 20 always succeeds
	* Add an appropriate MOD to your save roll.  Use the single best value if multiple options apply.
		* STR: resisting forced movement, grappling
		* DEX: dodging, diving for cover, keeping your footing, reacting quickly
		* CON: resisting poison, disease, body afflicting magic
		* INT: resisting magical spells (not all magical effects) with counterspells
		* WIS: resisting deceptions, subtle influences (charm), confusion, and disorientation
		* CHA: overwhelming will (fear, domination) or spirit (possession)
