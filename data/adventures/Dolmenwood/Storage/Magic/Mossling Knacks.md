### Bird Friend

* Bird Speach (1st): speak with birds
* Bird Companion (3rd): charm a level 1 bird (it saves vs spells) to be your companion until dismissed.  Can only have 1 bird at a time.
* Twittering Message (5th): 1/day, forest birds relay a message of up to 10 words, passed among the birds 2 hexes (12 miles) per hour to a specified person or location, where it is reproduced in a throng of chirps and calls.
* Summon Flock (7th): 1/day, call a flock of woodland birds (stat block in book); starlings, thrushes, blackbirds, etc.  It serves for 1d4 Turns.

### Lock Singer

* Open Simple Lock (1st): each Turn you sing to a simple, mundane lock, 2-in-6 chance of opening it.
* Locate Key (3rd): a brief, whispered conversation with a lock reveals the location of its nearest key.
* Snap Shut (5th): singing for a round causes all simple, mundane locks within 30' to snap shut.  While you sing, they won't open to pick or key, and magic must overcome you as per Dispel.
* Open Any Lock (7th): your song works on any kind of lock (2-in-6 oer Turn).  It works on magic locks, but when it would succeed, there is instead a 1-in-6 chance it backfires and seals your mouth for 1d4 days.

### Root Friend

* Root Question (1st): 1/day, pose a question to a root and it answers truthfully in 1d6 words.  Roots generally only know about their plant or surroundings.
* Summon Roots (3rd): 1/day, summon 1d4 rations of edible roots from the soil.
* Root Respite (5th): 1/day, roots draw you underground into their root system where you may lie for up to an hour.
* Summon Root Thing (7th): 1/day, summon a Root Thing (stat block in book) from the earth.  It arrives in 1d6 rounds and obeys for 1d6 Turns before returning underground.

### Thread Whistling

* You whistle to command threads and strings within 30'.
* Thread Mastery (1st): your whistle can tie, untie, or unravel any textile fiber thinner than a rope (string, lace, fabric).
* Animate Threads (3rd): threads move according to your whistled commands, moving up to 5' / round and moving up to 20 coins weight.  They can't attack.
* Rope Mastery (5th): ropes tighten or loosen at your command.
* Animate Rope (7th): a rope animates and obeys (stat block in book), potentially entangling a foe.  You must concentrate and whistle throughout, taking your action and slowing you to half speed.

### Wood Kenning

* Spend a Turn touching a a wooden item or living woody plant or tree.  
* Sense History (1st): learn the name of the item's creator or the last person to touch it.
* Sense Emotions (3rd): discern the most recent strong emotion of a sentient being absorbed into the wood.
* See Beyond (5th): get a momentary glimpse of what lies on the other side of a wooden barrier (wall, door, tree, etc).
* True Name (7th): learn a tree's true name.  Once a day, speak a tree's true name and get a glimpse of the tree's surroundings.  Invoking the name of a dead tree gives a curious vision of the vegetable afterlife.

### Yeast Master

* Ferment (1st): touch a sweet liquid and cause it to ferment (1 pint / Turn) into a frothy, alcoholic brew delicious to mosslings (2-in-6 it's appealing to others, though always drinkable).
* Commune With Yeast (3rd): sip from a partly consumed sweet or alcoholic drink and learn the drinker's name.
* Yeasty Belch (5th): 1/day, you emit a heady, fermented belch at a target within 10'.  They must save vs Blast or faint for 1d6 rounds.
* Yeast Feast (7th): 1/day, cause the omnipresent microbes around to grow into a yeasty feast of 1d6 rations (chunk of rubbery compact yeast, frothing sheets of slime, bubbles of alcoholic brew)
