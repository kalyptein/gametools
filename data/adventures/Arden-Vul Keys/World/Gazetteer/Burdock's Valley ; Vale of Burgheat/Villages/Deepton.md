
mining village in the foothills of the western mountains

half the residents are involved with the iron mine, the rest farm wheat, legumes, and barley

900 people

in antiquity, gold and silver where mined in these parts, and Lord Burdock wishes in vain to strike a fresh vein


the poisoners have contacts here who they meet with, posing as small traders from Gosterwick (coming down the north road), they drop off their supply, take their cut and get supplies to bring back.

the contacts have contacts with teamsters who make the run to and from Newmarket, often stopping at their hideout in the New Forest on the banks of the Gold River
