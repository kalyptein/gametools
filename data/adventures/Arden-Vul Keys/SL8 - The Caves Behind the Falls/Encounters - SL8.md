
Areas SL8-11 to SL8-24
* No random encounters.

In the southern section of the level, the inhabitants of SL8-5 or SL8-7 may wander.
* If PCs investigate SL8-3 to SL8-10 first: the Set guardsmen or the Set animals in SL8-5 may wander to the west.
* If PCs investigate SL8-5 first: some portion of the Set cult or Set animals in SL8-7 may  wander to the east.

* 1-in-6 chance every 4 Turns
